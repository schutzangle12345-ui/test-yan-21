Our Mission

Bridging the gap between AI hype and P&L reality.

test-yan-21 was founded to help enterprise executives stop "boiling the ocean" and start delivering measurable AI value in 90-day cycles.

The Problem

Why we exist.

We saw a recurring pattern in the enterprise: vendors overselling massive AI visions that take years to deploy, while internal teams launch siloed pilots that duplicate work and waste budget.

CTOs and Chief AI Officers are under immense pressure to show ROI, yet they lack the framework to tie technical experimentation to business outcomes. test-yan-21 provides that framework.

Our Approach

The 90-Day Mandate.

We believe enterprise AI strategy shouldn't be a 200-page slide deck that sits on a shelf. It should be a living roadmap of prioritized initiatives.

Every engagement is designed to identify, build, and measure high-impact projects that prove value to stakeholders within the first quarter.

Core Values

How we drive transformation.
